About Our APP

Health Monitoring has always been a point of concern. A wearable hand device is being engineered to collect real time data from the user and that data will be analyzed and sent to the linked remote device of the user for further reference.

This application has developed using modern sensor technologies, moder microcontrollers and a genius brain of enbedded softwares. This application, being simple provides lots of information that one could follow to build a healthy future. These specifications make this application "All in One".

The application has been developed for receiving the medical parameters which is displayed on our website with the help of sensors and at a the time uploaded onto the webserver of NodeMCU.

This embedded system is comprises of three major parts:

Sensors

The humidity sensor is used as a preventive measure in homes where people are affected by humidity.
The heart rate monitoring sensor along with a pulse oximeter. The sensor detects heart rate and to perform pulse oximetry.
The temperature sensor is extensively used to calculate temperature within rigid environments which includes mines, chemical solutions, otherwise soil etc.

Node MCU

The NodeMCU ESP8266/ESP32 will try to connect to your WiFi Network.
Once connected, it will display you the room temperature, room humidity, Heart Rate, Blood Oxygen Level, and Body Temperature, etc.

MATLAB & ThingSpeak

First of all, user needs to Create a Account on ThingSpeak.com, then Sign In and click on Get Started.
You can specify actions in Thing HTTP, which you want to trigger using other Thing Speak apps.
